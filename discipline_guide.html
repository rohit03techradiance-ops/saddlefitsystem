Choosing a Discipline

Pick the discipline that best matches the ride. The report uses this to weight rider posture, horse rhythm, saddle stability, and symmetry differently.

General Riding

Balanced default for mixed work, schooling, and general comparison.

Trail Riding

Prioritizes long-duration comfort, reduced drift, and calm rhythm.

Dressage

Emphasizes symmetry, seat stability, straightness, and posture precision.

Show Jumping

Focuses on lower-leg stability, balance through takeoff, and hip/knee control.

Eventing

Blends balance and adaptability across mixed work and forward motion.

Polo / Racing / Barrel

Dynamic disciplines where motion, control, and stability under speed matter more.

Selection rule

  • Choose the discipline that most closely matches the work being shown in the video.
  • If the ride is school work or mixed work, use general_riding.
  • If the ride is speed-oriented, use polo, racing_gallop, or barrel_racing as appropriate.
  • The closer the discipline match, the more meaningful the score weighting and recommendations will be.
