How to Click the 8 Points

Zoom in if needed, and click in this exact order on the first frame: saddle_front, saddle_rear, withers, croup, left_shoulder, right_shoulder, left_hip, right_hip.

Tips

  • Saddle front: Front-most point of the pommel.
  • Saddle rear: Rear-most point of the cantle.
  • Withers: Highest point of the withers (where the mane starts to rise).
  • Croup: Highest point of the hindquarters (top of the rump).
  • Left/Right shoulder: Point of the shoulder (front of scapula) on each side.
  • Left/Right hip: Hip joint area on each side (point of the hip).
